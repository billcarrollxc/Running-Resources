Drew Hunter


Drew Hunter is the eighth high schooler to run a mile in under four minutes, and the second to perform the same feat indoors, stealing the indoor high school national record from his idol, Alan Webb. After graduating high school, Hunter signed a contract with Adidas. His mile PR is currently 3:56.80. Drew Hunter is coached by Tom "Tinman" Schwartz.
